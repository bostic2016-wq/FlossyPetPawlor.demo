Common questions

Do you groom anxious or senior dogs?

Yes. We work at your dog's pace — no cages stacked in a loud room, no rushing. Tell us about your dog when you book and we'll plan extra time.

My dog's coat is matted — is that a problem?

Bring them in. We'll assess the coat honestly and talk you through options before we start. Severe matting may affect the price, and we'll always confirm first.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

Life happens — just let us know at least 24 hours ahead through your booking confirmation or by phone, and we'll move you free of charge.

Do you take walk-ins?

When we can! Appointments get priority, so booking ahead is the safest bet — especially weekends.
