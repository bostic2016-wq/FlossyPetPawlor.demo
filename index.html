FAQ

Questions we hear a lot.

Anxious dogs, matted coats, schedule changes — we've got you.

Do you groom anxious or senior dogs?

Yes — we work at your dog's pace. No stacked cages, no rushing. Tell us when you book and we'll plan extra time.

My dog's coat is matted — is that a problem?

Bring them in. We'll assess honestly and talk through options before we start. Price confirmed upfront.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

24+ hours notice via your booking confirmation or phone — we'll move you free of charge.

Do you take walk-ins?

When we can. Appointments get priority — especially weekends. Book ahead to be safe.
